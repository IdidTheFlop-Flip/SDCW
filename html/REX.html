How to build a REX

Description

today we will be constructing the famous robot spider thing from the hit game risk of rain 2

the robot might become hostile and act offensivley towards you

You will need:

  • 20 robotic parts
  • 15 pink flowers
  • a big ol' flower for the top
  • a camera with a torch (the eye)
  • 26 suicidal thoughts

Steps to build your own REX

  1. assable the robotic parts into 4 legs
  2. assamble the main body from robotic parts
  3. attach the pink flowers to the main body
  4. connect the big flower to the main body
  5. attach the legs to the main body
  6. connect the eye to the front of the main body
  7. now insult the robot until it has reached a horrible depression

Congratulations, You now have your own REX
